#1 Grilled Cheeze sandwich

  • Time: 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Chedar cheese 2 slices
White Bread 4 slices
Butter 3 table spoons

Step 1: Preheat a skillet over medium heat
Step 2: Slather one side of a slice of bread with butter. Place the bread butter-side-down in the skillet and add one slice of cheese.
Step 3: Butter another slice of bread and place it butter-side-up onto your sandwich. Step 4: Grill the sandwich until it’s lightly browned on one side and then flip. Continue grilling until both sides are browned and the cheese is melted.
Step 5: Repeat with the remaining ingredients for a second sandwich.
Step 6: Slice in half, serve, and enjoy!

Egg Salad

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Mayonaise 1/2 cup
Hard boiled eggs 8
yellow mustard 1 & 1/2 teaspoons
Thin sliced green onion 1
Finely diced rib celery 1
chopped fresh dill 2 teaspoons

Step 1: Cut eggs in half. Remove yolks and chop whites.
Step 2: Mash yolks with mayonnaise, mustard and salt & pepper to taste until smooth and creamy.
Step 3 Add remaining ingredients and stir well.
Step 4 Serve on bread or over lettuce.

Turkey Panini

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Whole grain bread 4 slices
Butter with canola oil 4 table spoons
Dijon mustard 2 tablespoons
Green apple thinly slice 1
sharp cheddar cheese 8 slices
deli roasted turkey 8 thin slices

Step 1: Preheat the panini press or grill. Spread the outer side of each slice of bread with 1 tablespoon Land O Lakes® Butter with Canola Oil.
step 2 Spread the mustard evenly over two slices of bread. Layer with apple slices, cheese and turkey. Top each with the remaining bread slices.
Step 3 Grill each sandwich for 3 to 5 minutes or until bread is golden brown and cheese has melted. Remove from pan, cut in half and serve immediately.
